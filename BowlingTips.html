BOWLING TIPS

The Chosen Ball

Make sure the ball you choose to bowl with iis not too light or too heavy to give you fine control. Make sure your grip is also comfortable.

Fancy Footwork

To give extra power to the ball, slide on the foot that is opposite to your bowling hand.

Fabulous Form

Make sure your shoulders and your hips are square with the pins. Try to get a visual line of where you want your ball to go and strike the competition out!

Don't lose your way

Do not step over the foul line and keep your eyes on the pins looking straight ahead. Then go for it!
